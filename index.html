Marilyn Monroe

A woman who captivated the world with her unabashed sex appeal

Marilyn Monroe was always happy when she had photoshoots, she smiled so brightly!

Here's a time line of Marilyn Monroe's life:

  • 1926 – Born in Los Angeles City Hospital
  • 1944 – Working in a factory as part of the war effort
  • 1950 – She became one of the most popular sex symbols
  • 1953 – one of the most marketable Hollywood stars,with leading role in three films: The Noir Niagara, Gentlemen Prefer Blondes and How to Marry a Millionaire
  • 1954 – Returned to star in one of the biggest box office successes of her career,The Seven Year Itch
  • 1955 – She dedicated herself to building her company and began studying method acting at the Actors Studio
  • 1956 – Got a critically acclaimed performance in Bus Stop
  • 1957 – Actinng in the first independent production of MMP, The Prince and the Showgirl
  • 1959 – She won a Golden Globe for Best Actress for 'Some Like It Hot'
  • 1961 – Her last completed film was the drama The Misfits
  • 1962 – She died at the age of 36 on August 5,1962 from an overdose of barbiturates at her home in Los Angeles
